About Us

Octo5 Estates Limited – formerly known as Legacy Realties Limited was incorporated in 1997 to explore the emerging opportunities in the Nigerian economy. Octo5 Estates and its sister companies have since become known as innovative companies in the drive to create unique communities and enhance the value of such developments through their bouquet of services. It is committed to developing and implementing innovative property and construction solutions that are tailor made to suit the need of Nigerians. Over the next five years, we are focusing on addressing the huge supply deficit and affordability gap impeding home ownership.

OUR CORE VALUES

We strive to deliver value at the lowest possible cost on all our projects. As good corporate citizens, we believe in the community and seek to add value to it through all our services. As professionals, we value trust and integrity and aim to reflect it in all dealings with our clients and contractors. As individuals, we believe in the sanctity of the environment. Any project capable of distorting the environment shall be modified to ensure compliance. We believe in the existence of a Supreme Being and strive to honour Him in all we do.

OUR PRODUCT

STOW (Save To Own Saving)

Home ownership in Nigeria is a huge challenge. Majority live in rented apartments with inadequate services. The high cost of development coupled with the difficulty in accessing mortage or credit financing makes owning a home seem like an unachievable dream for many. STOW (Save To Own Saving) Target Saving Scheme. STOW is a home savings scheme that aims to introduce starter homes which are vertical cities with lower development costs and higher rental yields in urban centres across Nigeria.
